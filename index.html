About

I am a metallurgist specializing in improving the fatigue performance and reliability of aluminium alloys through processing and microstructural optimization. My Ph.D. work on Al 6063 involved ECAP, T6 heat treatment, and deep cryogenic treatment, supported by fatigue testing, tensile characterization, and SEM/EBSD/XRD analysis. I aim to apply these skills to materials development, failure analysis, product qualification, and manufacturing process improvement in industry.

Location: India • Open to relocation

Research & Thesis

Ph.D. Thesis: Fatigue Behaviour of Al 6063 alloy in Heat Treated, Deep Cryogenic Treated, and Equal Channel Angular Pressing (ECAP) Processed Conditions
SASTRA Deemed University • Status: Thesis submitted
  • Problem: Enhance low-cycle fatigue and tensile properties of Al 6063 through processing–microstructure optimization.
  • Approach: ECAP (90°/120°), T6 heat treatment, DCT; LCF at 0.4% TSA across multiple frequencies; tensile & micro-hardness.
  • Characterization: SEM/EBSD/XRD; fractography and failure analysis.
  • Applications: Potential use in hoop frames, floor frames, and stringers as alternatives to Al 6061.
